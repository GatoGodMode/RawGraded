RawGraded Studio capabilities

Still capture plus guided video forensics and a local portfolio — all on your PC, with a vision model that is still being refined.

  • Guided video analysis

    A 5-stage scan walks you through front hold, left/right tilt, up/down tilt, macro close-up, and back scan. Forensic frames under tilted light feed edge and surface review — evidence appears on your certificate.

  • Local portfolio

    Save graded cards to SQLite on your PC. Search, sort, and archive your collection; attach provenance; refresh TCG and PriceCharting market snapshots per card. Nothing is hosted in a RAW ENGINE cloud portfolio.

  • Model in training

    The vision grading model is under active refinement. Stills and video frames help improve detection over time. Treat every grade and TCG condition label as a research estimate — not a slab submission.

Raw Investor

Microsoft Store or direct download · local API · optional browser extension

You on Windows ↓ RawInvestor.exe ↓ Local SQLite portfolio ↓ Loopback API — 127.0.0.1:3001 (your PC only) optional — you load & configure Edge/Chrome MV3 extension (unpacked, you choose folder) ↓ Market sites — only when you browse & capture
  • Extension is not from a browser store — you load resources\browser-extension-edge yourself.
  • You set Server base to http://127.0.0.1:3001 in the extension popup.
  • Captures post to POST /api/ext/capture on your machine — not to our servers.
